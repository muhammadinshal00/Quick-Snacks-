Sev Nimco

Sev Nimko is a crunchy and spicy Indian snack made primarily with thin sev (chickpea flour noodles) mixed with nuts, fried lentils, and spices. It includes ingredients like peanuts, cashews, roasted chana dal, and curry leaves for extra flavor. The snack is seasoned with red chili powder, turmeric, salt, and chaat masala for a tangy kick. Sev Nimko is known for its light, crispy texture and bold taste. It is commonly enjoyed with tea or as a quick munching snack. This savory mix is popular during festivals, travel, and gatherings. Unlike other nimkos, it has a higher proportion of sev, giving it a distinct appearance. It is vegetarian and can be customized to different spice levels. Store-bought versions are widely available, but homemade Sev Nimko is fresher and less oily. Perfect for satisfying cravings, it is a staple in Indian snack culture.
