Nimco Mixture

Nimco Mixture is a quintessential Indian snack that brings together an irresistible combination of crispy fried ingredients like sev (chickpea noodles), peanuts, cornflakes, roasted dal, and crunchy spices. This flavorful medley gets its signature taste from a bold blend of chili powder, turmeric, chaat masala, and aromatic seasonings, creating a perfect harmony of spicy, tangy, and savory notes. Loved for its satisfying crunch and addictive flavor, it's a staple at tea time, gatherings, and festive occasions. What makes Nimco Mixture special is its textural variety - from the light crispness of sev to the hearty bite of nuts and lentils. While readily available in stores, homemade versions allow for personalized spice levels and ingredient preferences. Typically vegetarian and often gluten-free, this versatile snack can be customized with additions like cashews, curry leaves, or even a hint of sweetness. Perfect for munching on-the-go or serving to guests, Nimco Mixture remains an all-time favorite across generations for its bold flavors and satisfying crunch.
