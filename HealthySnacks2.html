Apple Slices With Peanut Butter

Apple slices with peanut butter is a simple, nutritious, and delicious snack that combines the crisp, natural sweetness of apples with the creamy, rich texture of peanut butter. A medium apple (about 95 calories) is typically sliced and paired with 1-2 tablespoons of peanut butter (90-190 calories), resulting in a snack totaling around 185-285 calories. This combination is packed with essential nutrients, including fiber from the apple and healthy fats, protein, and vitamins from the peanut butter. It’s a balanced snack that provides sustained energy, making it ideal for a mid-day pick-me-up, post-workout refuel, or even a light dessert. The mix of complex carbs and healthy fats also helps stabilize blood sugar levels, keeping you fuller for longer. Easy to prepare and portable, it’s a versatile option for both kids and adults.
