Veggie Sticks With Hummus

Veggie sticks with hummus are a nutritious and delicious snack that combines fresh, crunchy vegetables with creamy, flavorful hummus. Common veggies used include carrots, celery, cucumber, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes, all cut into easy-to-eat sticks or slices. Hummus, made from blended chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, and garlic, adds protein and healthy fats to the snack. This combination is rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, making it a great choice for a healthy diet. Veggie sticks with hummus are perfect for kids, adults, or parties, as they are easy to prepare and serve. They are also a great way to encourage picky eaters to enjoy more vegetables. Portable and versatile, this snack is ideal for school lunches, picnics, or quick bites at home. Plus, it’s gluten-free, vegan, and suitable for various dietary preferences.
