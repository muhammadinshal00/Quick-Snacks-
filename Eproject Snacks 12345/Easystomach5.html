Boiled Potatoes

Boiled potatoes are a simple and nutritious dish enjoyed worldwide. They are prepared by boiling whole or chopped potatoes in water until they become tender. The cooking time varies depending on the size and type of potato, usually taking 15 to 30 minutes. Adding salt to the boiling water enhances their flavor. Boiled potatoes can be served plain, mashed, or seasoned with butter, herbs, and spices. They are a good source of carbohydrates, fiber, and essential vitamins like vitamin C and B6. This dish pairs well with meats, vegetables, and sauces. It is commonly used in salads, soups, and side dishes. Boiled potatoes are easy to digest and suitable for all age groups. They can be stored in the refrigerator and reheated as needed. A versatile and filling food, they are a staple in many cuisines around the world.
