Nuts And Seeds

Nuts and seeds are nutrient-dense, versatile foods that are widely recognized for their health benefits. They include varieties like almonds, walnuts, cashews, pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, and sunflower seeds. Packed with healthy fats, protein, fiber, vitamins (such as vitamin E and B vitamins), and minerals (like magnesium, zinc, and iron), they support heart health, brain function, and overall well-being. Despite being calorie-dense (a small handful can range from 150-200 calories), they are highly satiating, making them a great snack for weight management. Nuts and seeds can be eaten raw, roasted, or added to dishes like salads, yogurt, or smoothies. They also provide antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties, contributing to long-term health. However, portion control is key due to their high-calorie content.
